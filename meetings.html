Currently we try and hold meetings once every 4/6 weeks. We have three main types of meetings:

  • Speaking Night - several present a talk on a topic of their choice.
  • Hack Night - collaborate on a few projects or get help on your own project.
  • Social Night - over a coffee or a beer we discuss all things Elixir.

    • To stay informed about our upcoming meetings you can keep up to date with #events or #elixir channels on Nitech. Alternatively you can subscribe to the meetup page.
