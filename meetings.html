Currently we try and hold a short meeting every 2/3 weeks. We have two main types of meetings:

  • Hack Night - collaborate (mob program) on a project.
  • Speaking Night - several present a talk on a topic of their choice.

Hack Night

Our most common theme. We take a project and mob program around it. This gives all members a chance to code no matter their level of experience. We are currently implementing a Dead Man's Switch.

Speaking Night

If you are interested in Erlang / Elixir (anything that runs on BEAM) then and are interested in speaking, feel free to reach out to one of the organisers and we would gladly host you.

To stay informed about our upcoming meetings you can keep up to date with #beam channels on Irish Tech Community. Alternatively you can subscribe to the meetup page.
