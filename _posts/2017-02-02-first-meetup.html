Please come along to our first meetup, hosted by ShopKeep.

We have two great talks lined up.

Elixir for non-Rubyists

Maurice Kelly is talking about why Elixir isn't just for Rubyists who want to be more functional society.

Why OTP design principles are important

Steven Holdsworth is giving a brief tour of OTP and what it has to offer with regards to distribution and resiliency and why following them opens up a world of tooling and compatibility.
