--- title: About Us layout: post ---

Belfast Elixir is a Belfast-based community for anyone who is interested in the Elixir programming language, especially beginners.

We have a twitter channel @belfastelixir - but you can find us hanging out on the #elixir channel room on Nitech on Slack.

Belfast Elixir provides online and offline opportunities to:

  • discuss Elixir and related topics
  • socialise with friendly people who are interested in Elixir
  • find or fill Elixir-related jobs
  • We want Belfast Elixir to be a diverse and inclusive community, united by an interest in Elixir, and so we want anyone who is interested in Elixir to be able to participate
